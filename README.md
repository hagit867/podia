## Breaking Down the 5% Transaction Fee

Here's something that bugs some people: Podia used to market itself as having zero transaction fees across all plans. Now, the Mover plan includes a 5% fee on every sale.

Let's do the math:
- Sell a $97 course on the Mover plan? You're paying $4.85 per sale to Podia
- Make $5,000/month in revenue? That's $250/month in fees
- Make $10,000/month? That's $500/month in fees

At that point, you're better off paying $89/month for the Shaker plan and keeping 100% of your sales (minus payment processing fees from Stripe/PayPal, which everyone pays).

The sweet spot: If you're making less than $1,780/month, the Mover plan makes sense. Above that threshold, upgrade to Shaker.
